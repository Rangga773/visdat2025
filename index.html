Methodology

This visualization explores the economic value generated by quieter, calmer streets across UK Local Authorities. Values represent monetized benefits in £ millions, including improvements in health, sleep quality, amenity, and time savings. Data is aggregated by Local Authority and visualized through interactive maps and comparative charts.
